build: make
build debug: make debug

run: ./sj -p 1000 <arguments> <R> <S>

arguments (!!! datasets R and S must be the last 2 arguments):
	-p X: sets partitioning grid (1000 is ok)
	-c: creates the Raster Intervals for the two datasets
	-f: enables the intermediate filter that uses the RI
	-q: enables the refinement at the end of the pipeline	

datasets:
<R> and <S> can be selected from the following groups using these specific keywords:
	Original TIGER: T1, T2, T3
	N.America TIGER: T1NA, T2NA, T3NA
	Original OSM: O3, O5, O6
	ContinentalJoined OSM: O5Continental, O6Continental
	Continental OSm: <O5 or O6>_NorthAmerica, <O5 or O6>_SouthAmerica, <O5 or O6>_EurasiaAfrica, <O5 or O6>_Oceania

WARNING: Performing a join between two sets from different groups is untested and may cause problems.

EXAMPLE RUN:
make
./sj -p 1000 -c -f -q T1 T3
